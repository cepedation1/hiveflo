Getting Started
1Scan Your First Business Card
Go to the Scan tab → tap Upload Card → select a photo of a business card from your gallery or take a new one. HiveFlow's AI will extract the name, phone, email, company, and title automatically in seconds. Review the extracted info, make any corrections, then tap Save to CRM.
2Set Up Your Digital Business Card
After your first scan, your Digital Business Card unlocks automatically at the bottom of the Scan tab. Fill in your name, title, company, phone, and email. Upload your profile photo and company logo. Your QR code generates instantly — anyone who scans it saves your contact directly to their phone. Tap any share button to send your card via text, email, WhatsApp, or Messenger.
3Using Worker Bee AI
Go to the Bee AI tab. Ask Bee to write a follow-up email or text for any contact — just type "Write a follow-up email for Sarah" or use the quick prompt buttons. Bee AI knows your profession and tailors every message to your industry. You can also click a contact in the Contacts tab and ask Bee to write specifically for them.
4Connecting Your CRM
Go to Settings → CRM Connections. Click Connect next to your CRM (Follow Up Boss, HubSpot, LionDesk, or Salesforce). You'll be taken to your CRM's login page to authorize HiveFlow. Once approved, every contact you scan automatically appears in your CRM within seconds — zero manual work. To disconnect, click the green Connected button and confirm.
5Install HiveFlow on Your Phone or Desktop
On iPhone (Safari): Open hiveflo.app in Safari → tap the Share button (box with arrow) → scroll down → tap "Add to Home Screen" → tap Add. HiveFlow will appear as an app icon on your home screen.

On Android (Chrome): Open hiveflo.app in Chrome → tap the three-dot menu → tap "Add to Home Screen" or look for the install banner that appears automatically.

On Desktop (Chrome/Edge): Open hiveflo.app → look for the install icon in the address bar (a computer with a down arrow) → click it → click Install. HiveFlow opens as a full app window with no browser bar.

You can also go to Settings → Install HiveFlow and tap the install button there.
6Managing Your Subscription
Go to Settings → Support → Billing Portal to update your credit card, view past invoices, upgrade or downgrade your plan, or cancel your subscription. All billing is handled securely by Stripe. If you cancel, your account remains active until the end of your current billing period.
Frequently Asked Questions
What is HiveFlow? ▼
HiveFlow is an AI-powered business card scanner and CRM hub built specifically for real estate professionals — realtors, mortgage LOs, title agents, attorneys, and insurance agents. Scan any card, extract contact info automatically, let AI write your follow-up, and sync to all your CRMs from one place.
How accurate is the AI card scanning? ▼
HiveFlow uses Claude AI vision (claude-sonnet-4-6) to extract contact information. Accuracy is very high for standard business cards. For best results, take a clear, well-lit photo with the card flat and fully visible. You can always edit any extracted field before saving.
How does CRM connection work? ▼
When you click Connect on a CRM, you'll be taken to that CRM's official login page (this is called OAuth — the same technology used when you "Sign in with Google"). You log in and approve HiveFlow's access. After that, every contact you scan is automatically pushed to your CRM in the background. Your CRM login credentials are never stored by HiveFlow — only a secure access token is saved.
Can I connect multiple CRMs at the same time? ▼
Yes! You can connect as many CRMs as you want. Every scan pushes to all connected CRMs simultaneously. HiveFlow acts as your central hub — your contacts always stay safe here even if you switch CRMs.
What is the Digital Business Card? ▼
Your Digital Business Card is a shareable card with your contact info and a QR code. When someone scans your QR code, your contact is instantly saved to their phone. It unlocks after your first scan. You can share it via text, email, WhatsApp, Messenger, or download the QR code to print on materials.
What is Worker Bee AI? ▼
Worker Bee (Bee AI) is your personal AI writing assistant powered by Anthropic's Claude. It knows your profession and writes personalized follow-up emails and texts for any contact. Just tell it who you want to reach out to and what you need — it handles the writing.
How do I cancel my subscription? ▼
Go to Settings → Support → Billing Portal. From there you can cancel anytime. Your account stays active until the end of your current billing period. No fees, no penalties.
Is my data secure? ▼
Yes. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. We use Supabase for secure database storage and Stripe for payment processing. We never sell your data to third parties. You can export or delete your data at any time from Settings → Danger Zone.
What's the difference between Starter and Pro? ▼
Starter ($7/mo) includes unlimited scans, AI extraction, Bee AI, email/text drafts, 500 contacts, and your Digital Business Card. Pro ($15/mo) adds unlimited contacts, shared team CRM (3 users), pipeline tracking, CSV export, and priority support. Extra team seats are $5/seat/month on Pro.
Can I use HiveFlow as a mobile app? ▼
Yes! HiveFlow is a Progressive Web App (PWA). On iPhone, open hiveflo.app in Safari → Share → Add to Home Screen. On Android, Chrome will show an install banner automatically. On desktop, look for the install icon in your browser's address bar. It looks and feels exactly like a native app.
