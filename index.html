The Process

From Card to Connected

Four steps. Under 30 seconds. Every time.

1
Snap or Upload the Card
Take a photo of any business card directly in the app, or upload one from your camera roll.
2
AI Extracts Everything
Name, phone, email, company, address, role — all pulled out automatically and placed into an editable form. Review and adjust anything.
3
Outreach is Ready
A personalized pitch email lands in your email drafts. A text message is ready to copy. One tap to save the contact to your phone.
4
Saved to Your CRM Hub
Tap Add to HiveFlow — the contact is saved to your built-in CRM instantly. From there it feeds into your other CRMs automatically. One place. Every tool. Always in sync.
